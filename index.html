About SITM

Scholar's Institute of Technology and Management is promoted by the Scholar's Academy Education Trust, founded on 8th May, 2008 under the Indian Trust Act, 1882. Spread over a lush green expanse of more than 10 acres, set against picturesque surroundings, within a vibrant city as well as outside of it, endowed with all the modern amenities, yet in a natural amphitheatre, SITM provides an excellent academic ambience for intellectual activities.
